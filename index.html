Best Places to Dine in my opinion

  1. Zoom Café
  2. Java Joes
  3. Cha Cafe

Zoom Café

 Zoom Café serves Fried Chicken and Kimchi Fried Rice, which are my favorites in this restuarant.
Additionally, this is where my family usually dine in after church and get famished about it.
This restuarant never gets old and would Highly recommend.

What I love:

  • Their Fried Rice and Chicken
  • Affordable

Java Joes

 Java Joes serves bubble tea around my area and I hang around in java joes with my brother
whilst we do our laundry. I would recommend it especially it is relatively close to the college
and it is quiet so students can concentrate on their studies.

What I love:

  • It is close to the place I live
  • Convient when it is summer
  • Affordable

Cha Cafe

 Cha Cafe is the the first Restuarant I got into when I first arrived in Saipan.
It has the fondest memories I have in Saipan and My friends often meet up there
because it is the center of where we all live and it is in Garapan where majority
of the tourist site is there.

What I love:

  • The prize are reasonable
  • Near to the place where I am normally hang around.
